About Us

Who Are We? What We Do?

We are a group of recreational cyclists, who are dedicated to exploring and appreciating Tunisia together, and to finding adventures and beautiful places in two wheels, either on or off road. We welcome riders of all ages and abilities. We strive to always be exploring, learning, improving, laughing, and supporting each other.

What is Mountain Biking ?

Mountain biking is the sport of riding bicycles off-road, often over rough terrain, using specially designed mountain bikes. Mountain biking can generally be broken down into multiple categories: cross country, trail riding, all mountain (also referred to as "Enduro"), downhill, freeride and dirt jumping. The sport requires endurance, core strength and balance, bike handling skills, and self-reliance. Advanced riders pursue both steep technical descents and high incline climbs. Mountain bikers ride on off-road trails such as singletrack, back-country roads, and fire roads.

What Is The Word Punique ?

The Carthaginian civilization or Punique civilization is an ancient civilization situated in the Mediterranean basin and at the origin of one of the biggest commercial, cultural and military powers of this region in the Antiquity. Founded by Phoenicians on the shores of present-day Tunisia, according to the most commonly accepted tradition, Carthage gradually took the ascendancy over the Phoenician cities of the western Mediterranean, before swarming in its turn and developing its Its own civilization which results from the mixture of the autochthonous culture, constituted by the Berbers in Africa, and the culture that the Phoenician colonists brought with them.
